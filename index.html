Project Proposal

The project proposal is a detailed description of a series of activities targeted at solving a certain problem. Provide a logical presentation of a research idea. Illustrate the significance of the idea. Show the idea's relationship to past actions. Explain the major gap between existing projects with proposed system. Articulate the activities for the proposed project.
